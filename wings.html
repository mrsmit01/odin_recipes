Honey Old-Bay AirFry Wings

Ingredients:

  • Avacode oil
  • Chicken Wings
  • salt
  • black pepoer
  • cayene peper
  • smoked paprika
  • garilic powder
  • lemon
  • hot sauce
  • whorechester sauce
  • butter
  • honey

Process:

  1. Mariante the Chicken Wings with the 2TBP of Avacode oil, 2TSP of: Salt, Black Pepper, Cayene Pepper, Garlic Powder, Onion Poweder, Lemon juice in the refridgerator for 30 mins.

  2. While the wings are mariating, pre heat your Air Fryer to either the Wing setting or 400 degrees.

  3. After 30 minutes place your Wings in the Air Fryer and start the cooking process, you want to cook the Wings for about 25 minutes.

  4. While the wings cook we mill make the sauces!

    • In a skillet on medium high heat, melt a quater stick of butter.
    • Once the butter is melted, add about half the bottle of hot sauce of your choice, and start to whisket it in together with the butter, untill all combined
    • After the hot sauce and butter are combined you are going to add about 2 TSP of whorechester sauce and whisket that into your sauce, during this you would wannt to add any seasoning of your choice to the sauce
    • Place your skillet on low heat during, then you want to add as much honey to the sauce as you want and mix it into your sauce--be sure to taste the sauce and adjust hot sauce and honey to desired taste.

  5. Halfway through the cooking when you go to flip your wings in the Air Fryer

  6. once your Wings are done in the Air Fryer you are going to toss them in the sauce that you made and you are good to go!, you can garnish the wings with basil,parsmian chesse, and/or honey if you want. For a more crispier taste you can place the wings in the air fryer for 5 minutes after coating the wings in the sauce, after the 5 minutes re-coat the wings in the sauce and you are good to go! you can still garnish the wings if you want to !
